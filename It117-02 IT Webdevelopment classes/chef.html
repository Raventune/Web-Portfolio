The Restaurant is owned by Raven Clark, a young chef born and raised in the south who after cooking school decided to bring the techniques she learned to her hometown. While offering some hometown favorites, she also offers classic French recepies with a southern flair such as cornbread roulade, Catfish Flambe, and many others. She also provides a class for the poor which teaches how to shop and cook meals that are cheaper and healthier than what can be bought from the dollar section of a grocery store. Also offering tips for grocery shopping on an extreame budget.
