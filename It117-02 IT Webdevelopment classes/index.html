Welcome to the official website of the Southern Gourmet restaurant. We are happy to have you join us as we take you on a journey of great food, friends and family. You will love our Catfish fritters and our Pecan Souffle. We offer this and many more twists on southern favorites.

Be sure to check out our Classes section to find out how to make wonderful meals for you and your family with very little cash. In our classes, we will discuss how to shop for fresh ingredients that will allow you to fill your family's belly without lightening your wallet. Classes are free for low income individuals and families.
